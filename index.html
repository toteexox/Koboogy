Chapter nine: My Favorite Food My Story
▶️
What's your go-to Korean dish?
/wʌts jɔːr ɡoʊ-tuː kə-ri-ən dɪʃ/
네가 제일 자주 먹는 한국 음식이 뭐야?
▶️
It's hard to choose just one, but I think I would go with kimchi jjigae.
딱 하나만 고르기는 어렵지만, 김치찌개로 할게.
▶️
It's a hot and savory stew that has a distinct smell.
▶️
You can tell someone is cooking jjigae from miles away.
▶️
The great thing is that it's easy to make.
▶️
As long as you have good kimchi, it usually turns out great.
▶️
When you eat it at a restaurant, they usually cook it in a stone bowl called a tteokbaegi, which keeps it hot until the last bite.
▶️
My favorite restaurant, Sorojeok, sells kimchi jjigae for only seven thousand won, which is like five dollars.
▶️
My mom used to make it all the time when I was little, so the smell always reminds me of my childhood.
▶️
I crave it at least once a week.
